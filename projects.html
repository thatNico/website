    SDK Tools


    The nGine will come with various SDK Tools. Currently under development is nExus, the Map Editor!

    The picture above shows the nExus Editor. Maps are saved in the custom file format *.nwf which stands for nExus world file.

    As you can see, the nExus world file can be opened and displayed by the nGine.

    Note that nExus and nGine are completely seperate programs and don't share any data underneath the radar.
    You are always in full control of your files.
